Exhibitons

Our exhibition space is a captivating playground of innovation, designed to ignite the imaginations of both young and young-at-heart. Step into a world where interactive displays, mind-bending experiments, and hands-on activities come together to make learning an unforgettable adventure. Our exhibits promise to inspire, educate, and entertain.

Dinousaurs

24 May 2023 - 10 June 2028

Visit the creatures that ruled the Earth millions of years ago. See real skeletons and our lifeseize and 1:2 replicas.

EcoRevolution

24 May 2023 - 10 June 2024

In our innovative exhibition on the future of sustainable living, we invite you to open your minds and palates to a unique culinary adventure. Step into a world where insects take center stage as the eco-friendly protein source of tomorrow.

Bodymoving

24 November 2023 - 10 June 2024

See the intricacies of the skeletal structure and explore the astonishing network of the nervous system. Engage with interactive displays, 3D models, and immersive experiences that unveil the secrets of our biology.

Get down with the germs

24 November 2023 - 11 November 2024

A exhibition that explores the mysterious and ever-evolving world of viruses. Look into the realm of the microscopic, where these tiny entities wield both destructive and transformative powers.

Sandstorm!

13 Descember 2023 - 10 June 2025

Get hands-on with the tools of the trade, from turntables to MIDI controllers, and learn how music production has been revolutionized by computers and software.
