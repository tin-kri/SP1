Education

Our programs are thoughtfully designed to align with curriculum objectives, making them a valuable resource for educators. Whether you're a school looking to enhance your science curriculum, a club seeking an enriching outing, or a family eager to embark on a science adventure, we welcome you to our museum.

Robotics and programming

Our program is designed to empower children with the skills and knowledge they need to thrive in the digital age. Through hands-on activities and engaging projects, students explore the fascinating realms of robotics and coding, igniting their creativity and problem-solving abilities. With a curriculum tailored to different age groups, our class offers a safe and supportive environment for kids to develop essential STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) skills. Join us as we inspire the next generation of inventors, programmers, and problem solvers, sparking a lifelong love for learning and technology along the way.

VR Painting

Experience art like never before with our innovative VR Painting Education Program. Through the magic of Virtual Reality, we invite students of all ages to unleash their creativity in a whole new dimension. Our program merges technology and art, providing an immersive platform for budding artists to paint and sculpt in a limitless virtual space. Whether you're a school looking to inspire the next generation of Picassos or a family seeking a unique artistic adventure, our VR Painting program offers a truly unforgettable educational experience. Join us as we redefine the boundaries of art and education, one virtual brushstroke at a time.

Entomology

Get up close and personal with the delicate wonders of the natural world through our 'Mount Your Own Butterfly' experience. In this hands-on workshop, participants of all ages have the unique opportunity to learn about the life cycle, anatomy, and ecology of butterflies. Under the guidance of our expert educators, you'll carefully mount and preserve your very own butterfly specimen. It's a truly immersive and educational experience that allows you to appreciate the intricate beauty of these enchanting creatures.
